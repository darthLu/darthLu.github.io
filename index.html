About

Lindsey, originally from Lilburn, GA, near Stone Mountain, carries her hometown's spirit throughout her journey. Graduating in 2018 with a Bachelor's degree in Software Development was a significant milestone, made even more special with her son cheering her on. She began her career as an intern at Adult Swim Games during college, transitioning seamlessly to a full-time position the Monday after graduation, highlighting her dedication and skills. Currently, Lindsey holds a Lead Quality Assurance role at Blue Mammoth Games. Beyond software, she enjoys photography, crocheting, Sudoku puzzles, mountain biking, and riding motorcycles. Sunlit days, playing with her dogs, video games, and captivating reads bring her joy. Guided by empathy and integrity, Lindsey looks forward to unfolding her dreams with passion and values guiding each step.
