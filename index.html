About

Born and raised in Lilburn, GA, nestled near Stone Mountain, Lindsey has carried the spirit of her hometown with her throughout her journey. Graduating college in 2018 was a significant milestone, made even more special with her son cheering her on. Armed with a Bachelor's degree in Software Development, she embarked on a fulfilling career journey, starting with an internship at Adult Swim Games during her college years. The transition from intern to full-time employee seamlessly occurred the Monday after her graduation, a testament to her dedication and skills. This career path evolved into a Lead Quality Assurance position at Blue Mammoth Games, where Lindsey currently works.

Beyond the realm of software development, Lindsey embraces a diverse range of interests and hobbies. From capturing moments through photography to the meditative art of crocheting, her creative side finds expression in various forms. Her love for challenges is evident in her enjoyment of Sudoku puzzles, while the thrill of outdoor adventures beckons Lindsey to mountain bike and ride motorcycles. Basking in the warmth of the sun, playing with her dogs, immersing herself in video games, and delving into captivating reads are among the simple joys that enrich Lindsey's life.

Guided by the timeless principle of treating others with the same respect and kindness she seeks, Lindsey navigates life with empathy and integrity. As she look towards the future, her aspirations and dreams are waiting to unfold, each step forward guided by her values and fueled by her passions.
